Data Protection

MediScan AI is committed to protecting your medical information according to HIPAA regulations and industry best practices.

How We Protect Your Data

  • End-to-end AES-256 encryption for all data in transit and at rest
  • TLS 1.3 protocol for secure data transmission
  • Images are processed in secure, isolated environments
  • No permanent storage - all data auto-deleted after 24 hours
  • Zero-knowledge architecture - we cannot access your raw data
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

What We Don't Do

  • Never sell or share your medical images with third parties
  • Never use your data for marketing purposes
  • Never retain identifiable information beyond analysis
  • Never train our models on your personal data without consent

Your Rights

  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Access audit logs of who viewed your information
  • Export your analysis results securely
  • Revoke access permissions instantly

Compliance

This platform adheres to:

  • HIPAA Privacy and Security Rules
  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • HITECH Act requirements
  • ISO 27001 Security Standards

This AI tool is for informational purposes only and does not replace professional medical advice. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for diagnosis and treatment decisions.
